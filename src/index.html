About PayMaker

At PayMaker Africa, our mission is to innovate payment solutions across Africa. We strive to provide a convenient, efficient and affordable way for churches, small businesses, universities and other institutions to send and receive payments.

PayMaker provides value-added financial solutions and payment services that makes it easy to accept and make payments with ease and build a culture of saving.

PayMaker's vision is to be the most innovative, seamless and dependable online payment and savings provider in Africa. We aim to serve as a reliable partner to individuals and companies in facilitating the unification of financial technology into everyday financial activities.
